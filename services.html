Foam concrete

Discover the versatility and durability of foam concrete for your construction projects Insulation: Due to its lightweight nature and excellent insulation properties, foam concrete is often used as a thermal insulating material in buildings. Void Filling: It's used for filling voids in construction, such as filling abandoned underground pipes, tunnels, or unused tanks. Sub-base Filling: In road construction, foam concrete can be used as a lightweight sub-base material, reducing the overall weight on the underlying soil. Roof Screed: It's applied as a lightweight roofing screed to reduce the structural load on the building. Precast Panels: Foam concrete can be used to manufacture precast panels for various construction applications. Geotechnical Applications: In some cases, foam concrete is used in geotechnical applications, such as slope stabilization or as lightweight fill material in embankments.
